Welcome to The Fire Inside

OVERVIEW: 
You are a fire wielding mage sent to rescue a group of citizens from an underground dungeon.
However, you have been trapped inside with hoards of undead pouring in. It is your duty to
kill the hoards and protect your people.

CONTROLS:
- WASD to Move
- Hold left click to charge flame (longer charge means more flame shots)
- Control flame movement with mouse

GOAL: 
- Survive
- Kill skeleton warriors
- Avoid civilians (backpack old guys)
